Our History

THAARU COFFEE SHOP was born out of the love for coffee and the wish to create a perfect space where people can enjoy their favorite beverage together. The café started back in Kegalle /2003 as a small family business, which quickly turned into a neighborhood gem, thanks to our determination of finding the finest coffee beans and brewing every drink to perfection. From the start, we’ve known coffee is not just a drink more like a moment to feel.

So many years later without sacrificing quality and connectivity has been in our favor as well. We also ventured out of our café by selling coffee packaged for our fans to drink. But with each such advance, we have not forgotten our history—starting from relations with farmers and fair trade advocacy, and ending with the making of every single cuppa which in one way or the other depicts our story.

And looking forward, we will still strive to be at the next level of coffee without losing the community aspect. New taste, new technique, or new creators’ collaborations — it does not matter how exactly will we be changing as long as we will stick to the principles that have brought us here. Do not worry, wherever you are, physical location or virtually THAARU COFFEE SHOP will always remain your favorite place for great coffee and good experiences.
